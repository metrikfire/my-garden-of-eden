**Description**
also known as *epistemic regress*. Is a series of beliefs in which the [[1. Nodes/justification\|justification]] for each belief depends on the justification of the belief that comes before it. 
*Source: <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regress_argument_(epistemology)">Wiki, 2026</a>*

**Notes**
infinite regress problem is related to the [[1. Nodes/infinitism\|infinitism]] knowledge claim.
