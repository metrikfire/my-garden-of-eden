**Description**
Rhetorical question is a device that asks a question not meant to be answered. 

**Annotated Examples**
>”So the woman- who would be queen very soon, **for what is a queen but** an ambition pursued— took the sword from the girl’s hand and bid her kneel” — *Harrow. A, [[4. Artifacts/The Everlasting\|The Everlasting]]* (see also: [[aphorism\|aphorism]], [[caesura\|caesura]])
>
>*Effect: The sentence collapses political power into personal desire, exposing authority as constructed rather than sacred.*
