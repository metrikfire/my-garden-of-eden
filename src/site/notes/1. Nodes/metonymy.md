**Description**
A device which uses a word to identify or associate it indirectly with which it is closely related.

**Annotated Examples**
>That’s not history— that’s a story, designed to teach us who to hate and who to obey, **what god to worship** and **what flag to fight for** and what **color eyes** are the most beautiful. It’s a story that **made a continent into a kingdom into an empire.**” — *Harrow. A, [[4. Artifacts/The Everlasting\|The Everlasting]]*
>
>*Effect: **God**, **flag**, **eye color** stand in for religion, nationalism, and racial ideology.*
