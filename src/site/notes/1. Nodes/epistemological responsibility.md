**Description**
Epistemological responsibility refers to the ethical duty individuals have to ensure their beliefs are well-justified and to be reflective about the truth of those beliefs. It involves being accountable for how beliefs are formed and recognizing their impact on actions and others.


> **Quotes**
> Just as a gardener tends to the soil, we must tend to the quality of our intellectual inputs. Are we consuming a monoculture of opinions from a single [news](https://news.sustainability-directory.com/) source or social media feed? Or are we seeking a diversity of perspectives that can lead to a more resilient and complete understanding?* (Sustainability Directory, 2025)
