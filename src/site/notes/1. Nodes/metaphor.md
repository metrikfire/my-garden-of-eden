**Definition**
A writing technique that refers to one thing by mentioning another, in comparison or by contrast. 

**Annotated examples**
<blockquote>And I understood finally that a nation is a house with no windows or doors at all.That no matter what I did-- no matter how much blood I spilled in its defense or ink I spent in its praise-- it would never, ever be my home.</blockquote> 

~ *Harrow. E. A, Ch. 15, [[4. Artifacts/The Everlasting\|The Everlasting]]*
