**Definition**
A writing technique that refers to one thing by mentioning another, in comparison or by contrast. 

**Annotated examples**
>And I understood finally that a nation is a house with no windows or doors at all.That no matter what I did-- no matter how much blood I spilled in its defense or ink I spent in its praise-- it would never, ever be my home.
~ *Harrow. E. A, Ch. 15, [[4. Artifacts/The Everlasting\|The Everlasting]]*  (see also [[1. Nodes/allegory\|allegory]]) 


> A sound like someone falling through the air without ever touching ground, her voice pierced the thin walls with the hellish mix of a yodel and a howl. 
> *Vuong, Ocean, Ch. 3, The Emperor of Gladness* (see also [[hyperbole\|hyperbole]])
