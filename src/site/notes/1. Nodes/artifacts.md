**Definition**
**Artifact**: A result is said to be “artifactual” if it only holds in the special situation that you happened to test in your study. The possibility that your result is an artifact describes a threat to your [[1. Nodes/external validity\|external validity]], because it raises the possibility that you can’t generalise your results to the actual population that you care about.
