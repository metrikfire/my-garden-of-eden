**Description**
Behaving in the world so that you get exactly what you want, given the resources (physical and mental) available to you. Conversely, also characterized as the optimization of the individual's goal fulfillment. Differs from epistemic rationality in that is focused on the agent's goals rather than [[epistemic norms\|epistemic norms]].
