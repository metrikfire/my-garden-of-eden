**Definition**:
Interaction effect: An effect that occurs in [[1. Tags/randomized factorial design\|randomized factorial design]] when the influence of one factor depends on the level or category of a second factor (e.g., a treatment affects males but not females). The key word here is depends.

Interaction effects occur when the influence of one factor depends on the category of the other factor ( e.g., the treatment affects females more than males ). (pg. 16)
